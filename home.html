Things to Pack

Here’s a handy checklist of items to bring for your trip:

  • Passport and travel documents
  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Weather-appropriate clothing
  • Toiletries and personal care items
  • Camera or smartphone for photos
  • Travel adapter and chargers
  • Medications and first aid kit
  • Sunglasses and sunscreen
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Guidebook or offline maps

Travel tips

Make the most of your trip with these handy tips:

Plan Ahead

Book flights, accommodations, and popular attractions in advance to save time and avoid long lines.

Pack Smart

Bring versatile clothing suitable for the weather and activities. Don’t forget essential items like chargers and travel documents.

Stay Safe

Keep your valuables secure, be aware of your surroundings, and always have local emergency numbers handy.

Learn Basic Local Phrases

Knowing a few words in the local language can make interactions smoother and more enjoyable.

Experience Local Culture

Try local food, attend festivals, and explore neighborhoods off the main tourist routes for an authentic experience.

Use Public Transport

Learn the local transportation system. Trains, buses, and subways are often faster and cheaper than taxis.
